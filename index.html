Frenchcore

Frenchore is a subgenre of hardcore. the main difference between hardcore and frenchcore is the bpm. the average bpm in a frenchcore track is somewhere between 190 bpm and 250bpm. a lot faster then Hardcore. The drop is also a bit smoother, allowing it to sound better at higher bpm. some famous frenchcore artist are: DR Peacock, Sefa, Mr Ivex.

Frenchcore artist tend to use more real instruments in their songs instead of computer generated sounds, but this isnt necessary to classify as a frenchore song. a great example of such a song is Keelhauled by Sefa. This song uses the accordion as its main instrument. even when the drop hits, the accordion plays a big role as instrument.
